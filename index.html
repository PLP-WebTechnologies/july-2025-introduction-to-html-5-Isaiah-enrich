How to cook Ugali

The quick and easy guide to making Kenya's most beloved stable food

What is Ugali?

Ugali is a stable food loved in Kenya and many prefer it because of its nutrious value to the body. it is prepared by mixing different ingrediedents like maize floor with boiling water until it thickens.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of floor
  • 4 cups of water
  • a pinch of salt(optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil 4 cups of water
  2. Gradually add maize flour into the boiled water as you stir using a mwiko
  3. continue until the mixture thickensand pulls away from the sides of the pot.
  4. Cook for another 2-3 minutes while pressing and turning the ugali.
  5. Once fully cooked, mold the ugali into a round shape and serve hot.
Tips & Serving Suggestions
  • Serve hot with sukuma wiki, beef stew, or fried fish.
